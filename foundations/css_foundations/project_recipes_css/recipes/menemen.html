Menemen

Description

The favorite of egg recipes, the first thing that comes to mind, the savior of dinners, the joy of breakfast tables, and the reminder that it is the weekend, menemen is welcome to our table. The short, concise and economical ingredients in the recipe wink at our student friends, and their bellies are well filled.

For those who say "If I only have menemen, nothing will happen to me", the recipe is below.

ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 spoonful butter
  • 3 medium sized tomatoes
  • 4 medium sized green peppers
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoonsalt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili pepper

  2. Add 3 chopped green peppers and fry.
